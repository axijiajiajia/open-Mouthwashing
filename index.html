About Mouthwashing

Mouthwashing is a groundbreaking psychological horror narrative game that pushes the boundaries of storytelling in gaming. The Mouthwashing experience begins in a seemingly ordinary dental clinic in the quiet town of Millbrook, where players follow Jimmy's disturbing journey as he uncovers dark secrets that blur the line between reality and nightmare.

What sets Mouthwashing apart is its innovative approach to psychological horror. The Mouthwashing narrative adapts to your choices, creating a personalized journey of terror that transforms a routine dental check-up into a psychological descent into madness.

The Mouthwashing Experience

Players navigate through meticulously crafted environments, from the sterile confines of the dental clinic to the increasingly nightmarish landscapes of Jimmy's psyche. The game employs a unique "Trauma Response System" where your reactions shape both the narrative and the manifestation of horror elements.

Every playthrough offers new discoveries, with an adaptive AI system that adjusts the intensity of psychological horror based on your playing style. The core story features over 20 hours of main content, with additional side narratives waiting to be uncovered.

Mouthwashing Characters

Jimmy Morrison

The protagonist whose routine dental appointment spirals into a nightmare. A 32-year-old freelance writer with a history of dental anxiety, Jimmy's past trauma begins to surface during what should have been a simple procedure. His journey of self-discovery reveals dark secrets about both himself and the town of Millbrook.

Dr. Helena Swansea

The mysterious dentist with questionable methods and dark secrets. A respected figure in Millbrook with over 20 years of practice, Dr. Swansea's gentle demeanor masks a complex web of motivations. Her innovative "pain management techniques" have attracted attention from both medical professionals and investigators.

Nurse Anya Petrova

Dr. Swansea's enigmatic assistant who seems to know more than she reveals. Originally from Eastern Europe, Anya's presence in the clinic adds another layer of mystery to the unfolding events. Her cryptic warnings and subtle gestures may be the key to survival.

Daisuke Tanaka

A former patient turned conspiracy theorist who tries to warn Jimmy about the clinic's dark history. His obsessive investigation into past disappearances provides crucial information, but his mental state raises questions about the reliability of his claims.

The Story of Mouthwashing

The narrative begins in a dental clinic but quickly descends into a psychological nightmare. Your choices create unique pathways through the horror, as the line between reality and nightmare becomes increasingly blurred.

The World of Mouthwashing

Set in Millbrook, a town where beneath its peaceful facade lies a history of unexplained disappearances and medical mysteries. The Swansea Dental Clinic, established in 1985, holds secrets in every corner, waiting to be uncovered by those brave enough to look deeper.

Narrative Themes

The game explores deep psychological themes including medical trauma, trust in authority figures, and the nature of reality itself. Through your journey, you'll confront questions about personal identity and the reliability of memory.

Multiple narrative paths allow you to explore different aspects of the story, from investigating the clinic's dark history to uncovering a supernatural conspiracy that spans generations. Each playthrough reveals new layers of the mystery.
